Who am I?

My name is Ellaine Ho and I am a graduate student and working professional based out of NYC. By obtaining my Data Analytics Certification from Columbia University's School of Engineering, I hope to leverage my growing data analysis and visualization skills to help create insight-driven solutions in driving organizational and business performance.

Why am I interested in student loans?

As a financial aid professional, I've had some experience interacting with student financial aid, whether it be scholarships, loans, or other grants/fellowships programs. My unique background and knowledge in graduate level financial aid also piqued my interest in exploring student loan data, which is something that is more commonly discussed in the graduate landscape. With this project, I hope to uncover some insights as to how the student loan landscape has evolved over the past decade and to better inform you about the present state of federal student loans.
